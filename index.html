The Club Sandwich

ingredients

  • Bread - 3 slices
  • Mayonnaise - 1 tbsp
  • Eggs - 1
  • Tomato - 1
  • Chicken Slices or Turkey Breats - 2 To 3
  • Lettuce - 6 leaves

preparation

  1. Cook the bacon until it is crisp,
  2. Toast the bread and spread one slice with mayo,
  3. Layer on the egg, tomato and bacon then top with another slice of bread,
  4. add chicken or turkey, bacon and then lettuce,
  5. Finish with the final slice of bread,
  6. Once assembled cut into quarters and secure with cocktail sticks.
